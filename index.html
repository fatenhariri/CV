Period Work

2012-2015 Ph.D. student at LAL working within the ATLAS collaboration (CERN) with large data sets on Higgs analyses, missing transverse energy (MET) systematics and Higgs effective field theory using the Monte Carlo event generator MadGraph5_aMC@NLO:

  • Applied an innovative method not adopted by the collaboration to find new physics (see internship below)

  • Rectified some model parameters to allow correct event generation and automated a post-2016 version.
